Welcome to the
Traces of Online Presence

Explore browser fingerprints, their differences and similarities, through sound. A fingerprint is represented by a tetrahedron floating in space.

By moving closer you can zoom in on the sound of a particular device. You can listen to a group of devices by putting yourself at an equal distance from all of them.

You will start close to the fingerprints of devices that are currently connected to the exhibition, but you are encouraged to move out of this bubble and explore the sounds of devices that have connected to the server in the past.

You can enter and exit single fingerprints by flying into them.

Use the filter slider to see how unique your fingerprint is among the ones surrounding you.

This is a sonic experience, headphones are encouraged.



If you're having trouble with the sound on a newer Mac, try changing your audio interface sample rate to 44.1kHz or 48kHz. Click here for further instructions.
