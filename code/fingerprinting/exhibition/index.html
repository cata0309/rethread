How to navigate this exhibition


* Use the menu at the top left corner to exit at any time.

* Some experiments are sound art, so turn up your volume

* This is a collaborative exhibition, you can participate with your browser fingerprint (detailed on the next page), or just view parts of the exhibition that don't require collaboration.


Join our Discord chat to socialize during the exhibition! https://discord.gg/yfaMYt

Your browser fingerprint is everything a website can know about you without using cookies.

Hover over each value to see how unique your fingerprint is. Lower % means you are more unique. Higher % means you are more like the others.
